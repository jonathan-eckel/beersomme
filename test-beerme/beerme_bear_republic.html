Telephone: +1 707-433-2337

[Contact] [Map] [Update]

Notes

Opened: 1995

Annual Production: 1,500 bbl

Contributors: Arash Shahzadi, Kristen Tantarelli

Bear Republic was named Small Brewery Company of the Year at the 2006 Great American Beer Festival. Their production brewery is located in Cloverdale.
